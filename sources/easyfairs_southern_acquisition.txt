Title: Easyfairs acquires Southern Manufacturing & Electronics
Source: YouTube Interview with Helen Woodbridge, Head of Business Development, Easyfairs UK
Date: January 2025

Key Points:
- Easyfairs acquired Southern Manufacturing & Electronics after 10+ years of relationship building
- Southern Manufacturing is a 30-year-old family business founded by Phil Valentine
- Acquisition complements Easyfairs' Advanced Engineering event in Birmingham
- Southern serves the South of England (over 1/3 of UK manufacturing based there)
- Integration approach: keep existing team, add Easyfairs resources where helpful
- Founders Phil and Joe Valentine staying on as consultants
- Easyfairs now has 30 manufacturing/industrial events across 9 countries
- Deal driven by "plug and play" fit with existing portfolio
